The Market Opportunity

First-Mover Advantage in Untapped Market

Rejection Sensitivity Dysphoria (RSD) affects 99% of ADHD adults - devastating emotional episodes that can destroy entire days from a single perceived slight. Despite affecting 366 million people globally, zero tools exist for RSD intervention.

While competitors focus on productivity and education, DOSE addresses the crisis intervention gap - tools that work when ADHD brains are in emotional dysregulation, not just when calm.

We're not building a better productivity app. We're creating an entirely new category: crisis-ready cognitive infrastructure for neurodivergent minds.
